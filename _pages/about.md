## About me

I am currently a **Junior** at School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology. 

Nowadays, I was a Research Intern at [Research Center for Social Computing and Information Retrieval (SCIR)](http://ir.hit.edu.cn/), supervised by Ph.D candidate Longxuan Ma and Prof. [Weinan Zhang](http://ir.hit.edu.cn/~wnzhang/).

My main research interest is in Natural Language Processing. Currently I focus most on task-oriented dialog systems.
